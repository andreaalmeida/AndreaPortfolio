Who I am

I am an artist, a thinker, a doer, who loves to make connections to better understand the world around me. I'm a perfectionist by nature but I value rapid crude iterations. I don't like to do what has already been done. I need to create things that are unique, different, better. I fail a lot. I go through hundreds of different ideas until I land on the best solution. Sometimes it was the first idea that came to mind, but I needed to go through the other 99 ideas to make sure of that. I crave meaning in life, in everything that I do and experience. I love to create solutions that dazzle aesthetically but also make someone's task simple and more enjoyable. I'm a great listener, and I love to observe. I look for beauty in everything. Running is my outlet. It's where I reconnect with myself and my surroundings. It's my time to shut off my brain, to recharge, to be completely free, and choose the direction I want to go. But that is life right? We pick the path we want to follow. For me that path is design.
